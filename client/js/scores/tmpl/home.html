An Experiment with Javascript

As part of the curriculum for a Software Engineering Course, students from Utah State developed Map Editors, which utilized an API to send maps over to this engine.

See the Screenshots

Teamwork

Each student was put into a team of 4. Together they had to design, implement, and code their project into being. Valueable lessons were shared all around as the team grew and learned from each other. No man was left behind.

Expertise

Each member of the team had a job, and they did their job with skill. After carefully planning every model and view in their project, each team member went to town. Thus, were these intuitively astounding projects crafted.

Will

As in any post-apocalyptic, zombie-filled land; as soon as they hit, it was every man for himself. Forget what I said about teamwork. Just run man! Expertise? As long as you hit 'im in the head you're good. Don't look back. Keep running. And you just might survive.
